Pseudocode Outline - Everyday Task

How To Make Spanish Rice:

  • Heat 2 tbsps of oil over medium heat in a small pot
  • Add 1 cup of rice and toast the grains in the hot oil
  • Add 2 cups of water
  • Add 1 can of Herdez salsa
  • Add 2 chicken bouillon cubes
  • Turn down the heat to low and cover the pot
  • Let it cook for 25 minutes
  • Remove from heat, take off lid, and serve
